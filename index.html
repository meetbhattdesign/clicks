I photograph the world
the way a designer
reads a layout —
with intention.
Wildlife Street Portrait Landscape Gujarat eBird Conservation

Based in Ahmedabad. B.Des Interaction Design student at Anant National University.

12+ years behind the lens — photographing raptors across Gujarat's sanctuaries, strangers on city streets, quiet places between destinations, and the small rituals of cultures I've wandered into.

Wildlife got me started. People kept me going. Every frame is an attempt to understand something I can't fully explain.
